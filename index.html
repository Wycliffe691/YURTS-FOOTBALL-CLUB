YURTS FOOT FOOTBALL CLUB.

Football for Generations.

The Mighty Yurts.
Yurts Football Club, commonly referred to as Yurts is a professional football club in the Canadian Junior Football League - CJFL. The club is based in Leslie Beach, Canada. The club has won 5 league cups. Yurts F.C is one of the clubs with a huge support base in the Nation.
One of club's main social responsibility is to develop needy and less fortunate children having the passion to play football.
The club is also passionate about environmental conservation and aims at providing a safer world for evreyone.
